Team

Equity West’s principals collectively bring to the table a vast wealth of experience covering the real estate fund management and development industry.  With over 55 years of real estate experience and active management roles in over $1.1 billion in aggregate real estate transactions, the partners have come together to form a leading fund management platform.  The team has the breadth of knowledge, unique understanding of asset valuation, shrewd negotiation skills and a transaction structuring background to pinpoint, underwrite, and successfully capitalize on opportunities.  The successful track record of past endeavors, both as principals and in senior management roles, along with the personal and financial commitment as vested partners speaks volumes to the propensity for success of EWCP’s ventures.

Sean Douglas Robbins, Director

Mr. Robbins is a Managing Director of Equity West Capital Partners and focuses on the company’s capital structure and financing.  In addition to financial product structuring, Sean assists with assets acquisition and general project due diligence.  In addition to EWCP, Sean currently holds the role as President at Blackhawk Capital Group, a Newport Beach real estate advisory service and brokerage house.  Blackhawk Capital represents investors with acquisition of distressed and bank owned property at a discount with a niche for alternative financing methods.  Sean has facilitated the acquisition and funding of over $25 Million in properties thus far in 2009.  In addition to his role at Blackhawk, Sean serves as a member of the board of directors of Eco Land Holdings, an international real estate development company. 

Mr. Robbins has over 10 years of experience in real estate finance and senior debt lending.  He graduated Phi Beta Kappa at U.C.L.A with a major in Business Economics and a minor in Accounting/Public Policy.  Sean used his financial acumen to help Comerica Bank expand their commercial and real estate lending divisions.  Sean later joined GMAC-Cerberus Capital.  At GMAC, Sean successfully helped fund over $100 Million in development and construction financing for public and private companies.  In addition to holding a Real Estate Broker’s license, Sean is a CFA© Level I Candidate.
