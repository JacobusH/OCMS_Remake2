Welcome to EWCP

Equity West Capital Partners is a real estate investment platform with operations headquartered in Southern California. EWCP targets the acquisition of distressed single and multi-family assets with inherent value-add potential. EWCP focuses on the purchasing of distressed assets in bulk from Trustee Sales and a variety of bank and other financial institutions. Investors with EWCP can expect a low risk tolerance and above market returns through value add and off market transactions.
