Team

Equity West’s principals collectively bring to the table a vast wealth of experience covering the real estate fund management and development industry.  With over 55 years of real estate experience and active management roles in over $1.1 billion in aggregate real estate transactions, the partners have come together to form a leading fund management platform.  The team has the breadth of knowledge, unique understanding of asset valuation, shrewd negotiation skills and a transaction structuring background to pinpoint, underwrite, and successfully capitalize on opportunities.  The successful track record of past endeavors, both as principals and in senior management roles, along with the personal and financial commitment as vested partners speaks volumes to the propensity for success of EWCP’s ventures.

William H. Paine
Managing Principal

Mr. Paine is a Managing Principal of Equity West and has general oversight of all investment activities.  He is also the President of Volant Realty Partners, Inc. which is a full service institutional investment firm specializing in single family residential real estate assets throughout the U.S.  Volant’s services include research, acquisitions, asset management and dispositions on behalf of private investors; financial institutions; corporations; and other institutional investors. Volant is staffed with professional personnel who possess extensive knowledge and experience with respect to the single family residential real estate asset class concerning acquisitions; underwriting and due diligence; project and corporate finance; asset management and value creation; institutional research; and financial reporting. Presently Volant Realty Partners, Inc. manages over $160 million ($128 million in equity and $33 million in debt) on behalf of a large Kuwaiti financial institution. The related assets are developed single family residential lots within the Southern California market.

Mr. Paine is a former Managing Director of CB Richard Ellis Investors (CBREI) which is a global real estate investment management firm with offices in 11 countries and assets under management of nearly $40 billion. The firm sponsors investment programs throughout the U.S., Europe and Asia for institutional investors worldwide. Mr. Paine possesses over 30 years or real estate experience. During his 14 years at CBREI, Mr. Paine was involved in the creation, implementation, management and liquidation of commercial land equity investment funds with pension investor funding of over $500 million. Mr. Paine also was instrumental in forming and responsible for directing the CBREI residential investment programs with a total investor funding of over $150 million. Prior to CBREI, Mr. Paine spent the previous 12 years in various residentialdevelopment capacities including six years with Kaufman and Broad (now KB Home) as a Division Controller and Land Acquisition Director. Mr. Paine’s responsibilities included financial planning, accounting, forecasting, budgeting, land acquisitions, entitlements and dispositions.

Mr. Paine served as Acquisition/Disposition Director for Transcontinental Properties (a Bass Enterprises joint venture) with responsibility for acquiring on a national basis real estate entities/assets and selling land parcels within the master planned community of McCormick Ranch in Scottsdale, Arizona. Additionally, Mr. Paine worked for the accounting firms PriceWaterhouse Coopers

 

Eric Alan Paine
Managing Director

Mr. Paine is a managing partner of Equity West Capital Partners and responsible for the sourcing of assets for acquisition and the structuring of the transaction.  He oversees the underwriting and analysis of targeted opportunities and handles the management of real-property portfolio assets, including all construction.  He is also the President of EcoLand Holdings, an international development company working on what is slated to be the first LEED certified residential project in Puerto Rico.  In addition Mr. Paine is an officer and the corporate broker for Volant Realty Partners, a full service institutional investment firm with over $160,000,000 currently under management. 

Prior to Volant Mr. Paine was the Vice President of Pacer Communities, a private residential developer based out of Irvine, California where, in his 5 year tenure, he was involved in the development and disposition of residential units accounting for over $115,000,000 in revenue.  In his capacity there his direct supervision responsibilities included the teams ultimately responsible for all stages of a project’s development cycle – acquisitions, capitalization (equity, senior debt, mezz debt), design development, entitlements, and project management.  With his command of daily operations and acumen he served as the key liaison between Pacer and financial partners - equity partner Lehman Brothers, advisor Lakeside Realty Advisors, and various senior debt providers.  Prior to Pacer Mr. Paine was with Fieldstone Communities in Newport Beach, California. 

Mr. Paine has his BA in Economics with a minor in Accounting from the University of California Los Angeles, a certificate in Light Construction Development Management from the University of California Irvine, and is a licensed California Real Estate Broker.

 

Sean Douglas Robbins
Managing Partner

Mr. Robbins is a managing director of Equity West Capital Partners and focuses on the company’s capital structure and financing.  In addition to financial product structuring, Sean assists with assets acquisition and general project due diligence.  In addition to EWCP, Sean currently holds the role as President at Blackhawk Capital Group, a Newport Beach real estate advisory service and brokerage house.  Blackhawk Capital represents investors with acquisition of distressed and bank owned property at a discount with a niche for alternative financing methods.  Sean has facilitated the acquisition and funding of over $25 Million in properties thus far in 2009.  In addition to his role at Blackhawk, Sean serves as a member of the board of directors of Eco Land Holdings, an international real estate development company. 

Mr. Robbins has over 10 years of experience in real estate finance and senior debt lending.  He graduated Phi Beta Kappa at U.C.L.A with a major in Business Economics and a minor in Accounting/Public Policy.  Sean used his financial acumen to help Comerica Bank expand their commercial and real estate lending divisions.  Sean later joined GMAC-Cerberus Capital.  At GMAC, Sean successfully helped fund over $100 Million in development and construction financing for public and private companies.  In addition to holding a Real Estate Broker’s license, Sean is a CFA© Level I Candidate.
